Our research fellow, Gabby O'Connor, has joined the Te Whare Hēra studio space for a two month residency and has founded the bustling 'Ocean Collab Lab'.

Te Whare Hēra is a programme developed by Wellington City Council and Massey University that supports contemporary artists to develop new contemporary art projects. During Gabby's residency several projects involving the analysis of the water of Lambton Harbour within the larger Wellington Harbour/Port Nicholson are being undertaken.

You can read more about this residency, and the wider Te Whare Hēra programme, by visiting the [**Te Whare Hēra Residency page**](https://www.tewharehera.ac.nz/)
