Gabby O'Connor is an artist and scientist who is also one of our team members who is working on within the "Ocean Connections" project. Recently, she was chosen as one of the artists to help contribute a scultpure for the Sculpture on the Gulf 2027 exhibition, which is held biennially on the coastal headland of Matiatia Bay, Waiheke Island. This year, it is being held from the 26th Feburary to the 29th March 2027.

More posts about her sculpture and the Sculpture on the Gulf exhibition as a whole will come at a later date, for now you can see more about the exhibition and read about Gabby in the links below!
